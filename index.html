New York State of Mind

New York 311 housing calls.

A capstone project to analyse 6 million housing calls to the New York authorities and identify trends across the five boroughs. The Bronx topped the charts with the most housing complaints, with Staten Island having the fewest.

  • Date: April 2022
  • Qualification: IBM Professional Certificate in Python Data Science
  • Category: Data Science
