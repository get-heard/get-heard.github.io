Survey

Postal survey of unpaid carers in Halton

We carried out a postal survey of 1,000 carers in Halton with 266 responses. 62% of carers registered with Halton Carers Centre were providing care for more than 50 hours per week, profoundly affecting their own physical and mental health. Contact us for a free copy of the full report.

  • Date:December 2015
  • Client: Halton Carers Centre Ltd
  • Category: Survey

Asset Mapping

Mapping local charities to inform a Town Plan

We used Charity Commission data under the Open Government License to build a SQL database of nearly 200 local charities. From this we created a contact directory and a geographical representation of local charities according to income levels. This will be used to consult and engage with the voluntary sector locally on the Town Masterplan.

(Thumbnail map tile by Stamen Design under CC by 3.0. Data by OpenStreetMap under CC by SA).

  • Date: May 2020
  • Client: Southport Town Deal
  • Category: Asset Mapping

Project Review

Benefits assessment advocacy project

We carried out a three-year evaluation of a project which supported people with health conditions to get their voices hear at benefits assessment hearings across Lancashire. The evaluation used focus groups and semi-structured interviews to obtain feedback from clients, staff and stakeholders. Following the evaluation the project received repeat funding from the National Lottery.

  • Date: 2016-2019
  • Client: ICANN
  • Category:Evaluation

Data Analysis

New York 311 housing calls.

We carried out a capstone project to analyse 6 million housing calls to the New York authorities and identify trends across the five boroughs. The Bronx topped the charts with the most housing complaints, with Staten Island having the fewest.

  • Date: April 2022
  • Qualification: IBM Professional Certificate in Python Data Science
  • Category: Data Science
